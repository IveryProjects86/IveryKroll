Italian Sausage & White Bean Soup

Hi, I'm Sarita an aspiring chef and I wanted to share one of my FAVORITE recipes's with you.
Here is the list of ingredients and the steps to cook this delicious, flavorful soup

List Of Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian Sausage
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 4-6 Cloves, minced
  • 1 15oz can of Cannellini Beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 28oz can of Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Dried Basil
  • 1 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 6 cups of Chicken Broth
  • 1/2 cup Elbow Macaroni, uncooked
  • 1 6oz package of Baby Spinach
  • 1/3 cup Grated Parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste
  • Soup pot

Directions to Follow

  1. Heat up the pot over Medium/High heat
  2. Remove sausage from casing and cook until brown and no longer pink
  3. Add onion and garlic, cook until onion is tender and you can smell the garlic
  4. Now add basil and Italian seasoning. And a pinch of Kosher Salt here
  5. Add Chicken brother, beans and diced tomatoes. Stir everything together
  6. Cover and let simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  7. After 15 minutes you can add the elblow macaroni
  8. Cover and cook for another 8 minutes
  9. Once the Macaroni is fully cooked you can add the spinach, grated parmesan cheese
  10. Give everything a stir, allow spinach to wilt, and you are ready to serve
